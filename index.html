It all started with a cheap guitar and a will to learn some chords. Several bands and unsuccessful projects later, I arrived at my current full-time project HAVAQ. Georgia, USA native, I am simply a bedroom producer has been in groups like Dire, but I wanted to become a casual solo artist.

Drop-tuned guitars and aggressively powered acoustic drums mixed in with EDM make appearances on my work, as well as electro-focused synthesis and punchy, sampled drums. Constantly crossing multiple genres with every project, I'm always interested in trying new sounds. So one album may sound completely different than the other.

Although much of the music I release will not have a social topic, being purely instrumental, I do feel very passionate about liberty of the individual. Christianity is also important to me, and how God's grace is the only hope for this shattered world. Thank you for listening and I hope you enjoy yourself.
